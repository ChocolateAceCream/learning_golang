# Math Equations in Markdown

This is an inline equation: $E = mc^2$.

Here is a block equation:

$$
\int_{a}^{b} f(x) \,dx
$$

You can also use multi-line equations in block mode:

$$
\begin{aligned}
a & = b + c \\
d & = e + f
\end{aligned}
$$
